WeaponDescription=The Yu-3 is a Chinese acoustic homing torpedo designed to be fired from submarines. Several modifications have been incorporated to Yu-3 torpedo. One of the important upgrade was to incorporating ASuW capability to Yu-3 torpedo so that the submarines would no longer need to carry separate ASuW and ASW torpedoes.
