WeaponName=AEG-SUT
WeaponDescriptiveName=AEG-SUT Torpedo
WeaponDescription=The AEG-SUT torpedo is a weapon developed by AEG for use with the Indian Navy. The torpedo has active and passive terminal guidance capabilities, and can be wire-guided to the target, and is detonated by magnetic field. The weapon can also be used by the Type 209 class submarine, although is not fielded.
