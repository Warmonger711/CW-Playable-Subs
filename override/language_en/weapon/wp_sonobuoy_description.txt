WeaponName=Sonobuoy
WeaponDescriptiveName=Sonobuoy
WeaponDescription=Soviet aerial dropped sonobuoys equipped with either passive or active sonar. Helicopter sonobuoys: RGB-56 RGB-N Iva 'Crow Egg' (45kg, 2m) introduced 1956. RGB-64 RGB-NM Cinar 'Rook Egg' (13.5kg, 1m) (detection range of 1 to 5 km) introduced 1964. RGB-73 RGB-NM-1 Zeton (detection range of 3 to 8 km) introduced 1973. Fixed wing ASW sonobuoys: BM-1 RGB-1 'Snipe Egg 1' non-directional passive sonobuoy. BM-2 RGB-2 'Snipe Egg 2' directional passive sonobuoy. BM-3 RGB-3 'Snipe Egg 3' active-only sonobuoy.
